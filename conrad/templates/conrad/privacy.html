Privacy Policy

  1. What kind of data is collected?
    1. We collect data on all of the votes you make on images generated by the site.
    2. If you sign up as a user, we store your email-address. This is stored purely to allow you to reset your password if you forget it and will never be used to contact you for any other reason.
    3. Your email address will never be shared with third parties, and is stored securely.
  2. How will my data be used?
    1. The votes you make on images are used to generate new images which can be seen by you and other users.
    2. Your email address is not used for anything other than helping you to reset your password if you forget it.
    3. All voting data, associated with your username, both on in our database and in the gallery.
    4. The full set of voting data can be downloaded by any signed-up user. This data will not include your email address. The only piece of potentially personally identifiable data in this dataset is your username.
    5. This data may be used by any user to publish their own research on Genetic Algorithms.
  3. How can I opt out?
    1. If you would like your data to be deleted, you can email us and we will delete all of your data and associated votes and images.
    2. You can also use the site as a guest, and no personally identifiable information will be stored.
  4. Is your Privacy Policy GDPR Compliant?
    1. Yes. No personally identifiable information will be processed unnecessarily, shared with third parties, or stored outside of the EU.
    2. Our Privacy Policy is updated regularly. You can review it here at any time.
